About Us

Founding Members

The individuals who laid the foundation of this organization.

ANGAT’s Founding Members are a group of committed student leaders who came together with a shared vision of reducing poverty through education, empowerment, and community-driven action. They laid the groundwork for the organization’s values, programs, and long-term mission of creating equal opportunities for underserved communities.
