About Us

Doctrines, Teachings & Core Values

The principles and beliefs that guide everything we do.

This section outlines the guiding principles of ANGAT, including its core values, doctrines, and teachings that shape its approach to poverty alleviation. Together, they serve as a unified framework for promoting equity, empowerment, and sustainable community development.

Doctrines

The foundational truths that define our identity and purpose.

A

Access to Opportunities

Education and opportunity are fundamental rights and the strongest tools in combating poverty. We are committed to eliminating barriers by providing financial support, learning resources, and inclusive access to education and development programs.

N

Nurturing Communities

Human development must address both academic and personal well-being. We support individuals holistically through nutrition programs, mental health support, and community care initiatives to ensure readiness for learning and growth.

G

Growth and Development

True development extends beyond formal education. We emphasize practical competencies such as financial literacy, entrepreneurship, digital skills, and vocational training to enhance employability and independence.

A

Action for Change

Meaningful change requires active participation. We empower individuals, especially the youth, to engage in community-driven initiatives that directly address poverty and promote social development.

T

Transforming Lives

Our ultimate goal is long-term, generational transformation. Every initiative is designed to uplift individuals not only in the present but also to equip them to improve their families and communities in the future.

Teachings

Wisdom and guidance passed down through our organization's practice.

01

Value Education as a Pathway to Opportunity

Education must be treated as a powerful tool for breaking the cycle of poverty. Commitment, attendance, and discipline are essential in maximizing its benefits.

02

Prepare for Sustainable Livelihoods

Learning should extend beyond theory. Individuals are encouraged to develop financial literacy, entrepreneurial thinking, and technical skills to achieve long-term economic stability.

03

Prioritize Holistic Well-being

Effective learning requires physical, emotional, and mental stability. Individuals should actively support their well-being and foster supportive environments for others.

04

Serve and Lead in the Community

Leadership is demonstrated through service. Individuals are encouraged to participate in and lead initiatives that directly address community challenges.

05

Build for Long-Term Impact

Actions should be guided by responsibility and foresight. Every initiative should contribute to sustainable development and lasting community improvement.

Core Values

The values we uphold in every action and decision.

01

Equity (Katarungan)

We uphold the principle that every individual, regardless of socioeconomic background, deserves equal access to opportunities for growth and success. Our goal is to remove systemic barriers and create a fair environment where everyone can thrive.

02

Empowernment (Pagtaguyod)

We believe in enabling individuals and communities to become self-reliant. Rather than fostering dependency, we prioritize capacity-building, resilience, and the development of sustainable life skills.

03

Solidarity (Bayanihan)

We recognize that poverty is a collective challenge that requires collective action. We value cooperation, shared responsibility, and strong community partnerships in addressing social and economic inequality.

04

Proactivity (Kusa)

We commit to taking initiative in identifying and addressing societal challenges. We do not wait for change; we actively create and lead interventions that respond to community needs.

05

Sustainability (Pagpanatili)

We ensure that all efforts are designed for long-term impact. Our programs aim to create lasting educational, economic, and social opportunities that break the cycle of poverty across generations.
