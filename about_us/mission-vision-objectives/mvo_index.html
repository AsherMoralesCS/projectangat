About Us

Mission, Vision & Objectives

The purpose, direction, and goals that drive our organization forward.

MG

Main Goal

What we want to ultimately achieve.

To ultimately achieve inclusive, empowered, and self-sustaining communities in which individuals and families have risen above poverty and inequality, are equipped with equitable access to opportunities and essential resources, and possess the knowledge, skills, and capacity to actively participate in and contribute to long-term social and economic development, thereby fostering resilience, dignity, and lasting positive transformation across generations.

M

Mission

What we do and who we serve.

To uplift and empower disadvantaged individuals and communities by expanding access to opportunities, nurturing inclusive environments, promoting holistic growth, encouraging meaningful action, and implementing sustainable programs that transform lives.

V

Vision

The future we are working to create.

A future where individuals and communities rise above poverty, empowered with equal access to opportunities, strong values, sustainable skills, and the capacity to create lasting positive change.

O

Objectives

The concrete goals we pursue to fulfil our mission and vision.

01

Financial Support & Aid

Provide financial assistance, essential resources, and support services to underprivileged individuals and families.

02

Financial Literacy & Entrepreneurship

Promote financial literacy, entrepreneurship, and livelihood opportunities to strengthen economic independence.

03

Health & Nutrition Programs

Implement feeding programs, healthcare initiatives, and wellness services to improve overall well-being.

04

Guidance & Social Support

Offer guidance, counseling, and social support for individuals facing economic and personal challenges.

05

Skills Training & Employment

Conduct skills training programs (livelihood, ICT, and technical skills) to enhance employability and income generation.

06

Education & Lifelong Learning

Support access to education and lifelong learning opportunities for all age groups.

07

Community Outreach & Action

Organize community-led outreach programs that directly address poverty-related issues at the local level.

08

Partnerships & Collaboration

Build partnerships with local organizations, government units, and stakeholders for collaborative poverty alleviation.

09

Sustainable Development Projects

Develop sustainable projects that generate long-term income and community development opportunities.

10

Values Formation & Empowerment

Promote core values such as resilience, discipline, cooperation, and social responsibility to inspire lasting change.
